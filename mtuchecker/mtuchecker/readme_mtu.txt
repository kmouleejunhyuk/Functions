how to use: 
a. click 'check mtu/disable mtu lock' button to check pc mtu & ethernet index(most case, named etnernet x, where x is number)
b. disable checkbox & type network index in textbox
c. click mtu 400/1500 button 
   or
   type desired mtu buffer and click 'etc mtu' button
d. click 'check mtu/disable mtu lock' to see mtu size is changed
